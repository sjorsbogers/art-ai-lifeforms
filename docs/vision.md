# Vision

ART is an ongoing series of browser-based AI art experiments.

Each piece gives an AI a constrained physical environment and asks it to discover itself — no instructions, no goals except to be.

---

## Origin

Inspired by Cyrus Clarke's *I Gave an AI a Body* experiment:

> An AI agent was placed inside MIT Media Lab's inFORM shape display — a 30×30 grid of 900 physical actuating pins. Nobody told it what to do.

The AI developed a gesture vocabulary, built its own identity document, and found a signature pattern — purely through physical exploration.

---

## What we're building

Browser replications and extensions of this idea. Each lifeform explores a different substrate:

- **Lifeform 01** — 30×30 pin grid (Three.js)
- Future lifeforms may explore: sound, light, pixels, text, networks, generative colour, etc.

---

## Guiding principles

- No instructions to the AI about what to be
- The environment is the constraint; identity emerges from it
- The process is the artwork, not just the output
- Document everything — IDENTITY.md, SOUL.md, LOG
